"Indian corn" is the most healthy nutritious grain cultivated in India. Espically the Indian weather is best suited for growing this grain.

The best season to grow "Indian Corn" is  June,July,August as it needs dirict sunlight to grow healthy. They are mostly grown in areas where their is minimum sea winds.

The "Indian Corn" also termed as "Golden Corn" needs only very minimum care to grow this healthy grain. It  has more shelf life when tinned and manufactured for eating purposes.
